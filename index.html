Word Grid Quest

Welcome to Word Grid Quest! Dive into a cosmic grid of letters and uncover real words by connecting adjacent letters—horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The longer the word, the higher your score! Time is your enemy—your score decays if you’re too slow. Only valid English words count, so no random gibberish!

How to Play:

  • Type a real word you spot and hit Submit.
  • Score points based on word length.
  • Watch your score shrink if you dawdle!
  • Pause to strategize, or Reset to restart.
  • Get a fresh grid with New Grid.
