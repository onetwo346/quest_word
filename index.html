Word Grid Quest

Welcome to Word Grid Quest! Dive into a galactic grid of letters, connecting them horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to form real words. Longer words = bigger points! Beat the clock before your score fades into the void.

How to Play:

  • Spot a word, type it, hit Submit.
  • Score based on word length.
  • Score decays if you dawdle!
  • Pause to strategize, Reset to reboot.
  • New Grid for a fresh cosmos.

Ready to conquer the word universe? Blast off!
